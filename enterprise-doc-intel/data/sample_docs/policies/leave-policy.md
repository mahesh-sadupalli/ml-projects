# Leave and Time-Off Policy

## Annual Leave
- Full-time employees: 28 days paid annual leave per calendar year
- Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on contracted hours
- Carry-over: Up to 5 unused days may be carried into Q1 of the following year
- Minimum booking: Leave requests of 5+ consecutive days require 2 weeks advance notice

## Sick Leave
- Employees receive up to 30 days paid sick leave per year
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Return-to-work interviews are conducted after absences of 5+ days
- Long-term illness: After 30 days, employees transition to company disability insurance

## Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks fully paid leave
- Additional unpaid leave: Up to 12 months with job protection
- Gradual return: Part-time arrangements available for 3 months post-return

## Public Holidays
The company observes 10 public holidays per year based on the employee's primary work location. A full schedule is published in January each year.

## Special Leave
- Bereavement: 5 days for immediate family, 2 days for extended family
- Wedding: 3 days (employee's own wedding)
- Moving: 1 day per calendar year
- Jury duty: Full pay for the duration of service
- Volunteer days: 2 paid volunteer days per year

## Requesting Leave
1. Submit requests through the HR portal (minimum 1 week in advance for planned leave)
2. Manager approval required within 3 business days
3. Team coverage must be arranged before leave begins
4. Emergency leave: Notify manager by phone or Slack as soon as possible

## Approval Authority
- Up to 5 days: Direct manager
- 5-15 days: Department head
- 15+ days: HR Director
